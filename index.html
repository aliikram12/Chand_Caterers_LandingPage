Our Story

A Legacy of Flavor Since 1988

Founded in 1988 as a small food stall in London, Chand Caterers has grown into one of the UK's most trusted halal catering companies. Now serving across the UK with premium halal catering for over 35 years, we remain family-owned and operated.
